Welcome to Stuff Saver

A web-based app that lets you save and store all sort of things you learn

About Stuff Saver

If you are curious like me, chances are you learn tons of stuff everyday. Whether it is a new word or expression you come across while you're listening to your favorite podcast or reading the news, a video on YouTube you find extremely useful, or that grammar/spelling rule you should never forget. And yet, in a month or two, you're probably not going to remember all of this. That's where Stuff Saver comes in handy!

How it works

Create a new account by clicking on Register on the top right corner. Once you are logged in, add some stuff in the Add new section. Click on the dropdown Choose a category and either add some New Content or add stuff to a category you previously created.

Check your stuff in the Summary page

Everything you added is in the Summary page.
